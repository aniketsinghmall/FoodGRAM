Hello, welcome to FoodGram!

FoodGram lets you use User reviews to make purchasing food easy. 
The friends you have for the site are us: ChrisVat, Ryan Campbell, MattyP, Aniket S and Grafar P.

You can click our reviews to instantly view the restaurant or food item which we reviewed!
If you don't like our taste in food, you can opt to see the trending reviews instead which are from the most influential FoodGram users. 

You can even add your own reviews, if you aren't logged in they will be added under AnonymousUser. 

The file to run foodgram is index.html. 
Enjoy your time!
